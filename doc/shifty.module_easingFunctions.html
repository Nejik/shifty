Module: easingFunctions

Shifty implements Robert Penner's easing equations, as adapted from Scripty2's implementation. These functions are used internally by Shifty, but they can also be accessed externally via shifty.Tweenable.formulas.

See the Pen Shifty - Easing formula names by Jeremy Kahn (@jeremyckahn) on CodePen.
